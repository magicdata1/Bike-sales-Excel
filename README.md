# Bike Sales Analytics Dashboard  
A complete end‑to‑end data analytics project exploring global bike sales across multiple years, customer segments, and regions. This project includes data cleaning, transformation, pivot tables, interactive Excel dashboards, and visualisation assets.

---

## Project Overview  
This repository contains everything needed to analyse and visualise bike sales performance:

- Cleaned and transformed dataset  
- PivotTables exploring customer demographics  
- Interactive Excel dashboard  
- Power BI / Tableau‑ready CSV  
- Visual insights across revenue, profit, units sold, and customer segmentation  

---

## Data Preparation  
Key steps performed:

- Cleaned missing values and validated numeric fields  
- Standardised column names  
- Converted dates into usable formats (Year, Month, Day fields)  
- Created demographic groupings (Age Group, Customer Gender)  
- Exported a tidy CSV for BI tools

## Business Insights (Brief)

- Adults (35–64) buy the most bikes and generate the highest revenue. They are the main target market.
- Young Adults (25–34) also buy a lot and show strong growth potential in the future.
- Youth (<25) buy the least but represent early interest and long‑term opportunity.
- The United States and Australia are the top‑performing countries with the highest order volumes.
- Female customers often purchase as many or more bikes than male customers in several age groups.
- Mountain Bikes sell the best across almost every demographic.
- Sales tend to increase toward the end of the year (Q4) due to holidays and promotions.
- Older age groups tend to buy higher‑priced bikes, while younger groups prefer affordable models.
- Different countries show different buying patterns, which can support targeted marketing and stock decisions.
